Some Info On My Cats

I am following suggested advise and making my solo build website about my cats.


In order of Greatness, the cats I have owned as an adult are:

  1. Morris
  2. Butter
  3. Padington

Butter

Butter's favorite thing is to play with packing paper. She also loves hunting, mostly rodents. She once caught a crow (it was as big as her).


Padington

Padington is new to us, we've only had him about two weeks now. He will only eat wet food. He is black. He likes the cat tower we got for him, so that worked out well.


Morris

Sadly, Morris passed away in November (of 2018). He was the greatest cat of all time, beloved by his owners as well as most of our neighbors. He loved fighting and he once fought off a coyote.


Cat Problems

As much as I love my cats, they are living things that have issues and don't always follow the instructions I give them. The following is a list of issues with/complications of owning cats:

  • they puke sometimes, sometimes on things they shouldn't
  • cats think expensive new couch is expensive new scratching post
  • they require paid supervision when owners go out of town
  • They bring me dead things to eat that I don't want to eat
  • They don't always understand about the importance of 8 hours of sleep
